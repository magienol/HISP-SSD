About US

HISP South Sudan

HISP SSD Health Information System Program is a not-for-profit Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) specialized in designing and implementing solutions in health informatics for the public health sector. The organization is the only certified implementer of District Health Information Software (DHIS2) in South Sudan and subscribes to and supports the broader HISP agenda of creating “networks of actions” which seeks to strengthen collaborative efforts through research, monitoring, evaluation, learning and implementation of health information systems. We work in collaboration with Ministry of Health, higher institutions of learning, NGOs, implementing and development partners to support Health Information Systems and data utilization. The organization looks plans to work with other Ministries in the field of information technology platform developments for evidence-based decision making.
